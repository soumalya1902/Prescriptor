Welcome to Prescriptor

Prescriptor is smart medical prescription generator, created for doctors to aid them creating prescriptions. Our Machine learning model uses the recoded audio of the prescription and identifies valuable information, which then is converted into a digital copy of the prescription.

We have divided the prescription into four sections: Patient information, Diagnosis, Medicine prescribed and Advice to the patient. For each section recoed the audio and click on save. You can also edit the prescription manually in case. Then a digital copy of prescription generated, which can be accesses from anywhere using authenticated URL. Prescriptor also provides facility to send the digital prescription url through SMS free of cost.
